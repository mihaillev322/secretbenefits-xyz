Credit System and Pricing Structure

The financial model of Secret Benefits revolves around a credit purchasing system. Unlike subscription-based dating sites, users buy credits in packages and spend them on various platform activities. Sending a message costs 10 credits, while unlocking private photos requires 30 credits. This pay-per-action model means users only pay for features they actually use.

Credit packages range from 100 credits for approximately $59 to 1,000 credits for $289. The platform frequently offers first-time buyer discounts, sometimes reducing the initial package cost by 40%.
